Blue Whale Game

The Blue whale Game also known as "blue Whale blue-whale-challenge", is an Internet "game" that is claimed to exist in several countries. The game allegedly consists of a series of tasks assigned to players by administrators during a 50-day period, with the final challenge requiring the player to commit suicide.
